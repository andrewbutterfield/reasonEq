Theory Closure
Needs
  Exists Forall Equal Impl AOI And Or Not EQV
END







Law uclose_def BEGIN
 axiom
 ,
eqv CS (CLOSURE universal P,QS forall x$
  @  P)
,
saveSideCond NYI
END

 ,
eqv CS (CLOSURE existential P,not CS (CLOSURE universal not CS (P)))
END
